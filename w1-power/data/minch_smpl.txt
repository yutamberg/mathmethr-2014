Случайная стратифицированная выборка по 2 площадки с каждой зоны литорали.
Исходные данные из книги Quinn, Keough, 2002 Design and Analysis for Biologists (Box 9-5, Fig 9-7)

Format of the files:
	* minch_smpl.xls (Microsoft Excel)

File contents:
	* SITE - site label
	* ZONE - zones running up the shore (SZ(+TR)=seaward zone with mangrove trees, SZ(-TR)=seaward zone without mangrove trees, MZ=middle zone with trees, LZ=landward zone at upper levels)
	* LIMPT - average number of limpets on oyster shells per quadrat
	* LIMPT100 - number of limpets per 100 oyster shells
	* SQLIM100 - square root transformation of LIMPT100
